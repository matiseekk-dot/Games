=== GOOGLE PLAY — FULL DESCRIPTION (EN, max 4000 chars) ===

PS5 Vault is your personal PlayStation 5 game collection manager.
Track every game — from backlog to active play, completion, or sale.

🎮 COLLECTION
• Add games manually or search via RAWG database (800,000+ titles)
• Auto-fill cover art, genre, and release date
• 4 statuses: Playing / Completed / Planning / Abandoned
• Search and filter by status

📅 RELEASES
• Track upcoming releases with day countdown
• Notifications 3 days before and on release day
• Dynamic CTAs — before release: watch & plan purchase; after release: start playing or add to collection
• "Already available" section for released games you haven't bought yet

💰 FINANCES
• Log how much you paid and where
• Track game sales and calculate your real collection cost
• Cost per hour of gameplay
• ROI analysis — which game was your best investment

📊 STATS & ANALYSIS
• Charts: collection status, top genres, rating histogram
• Spending by store and genre
• Smart financial insights:
  — Biggest loss on resale
  — Best investment
  — Most expensive hours
  — Best value (PLN/h)

🏠 HOME SCREEN
• "Continue playing" — active game with progress bar
• "What to play next" — recommendation based on your preferences
• "Next release" — countdown with action buttons
• "Financial insight" — real collection cost and suggestions

✅ PRIVACY & SECURITY
• Zero registration — works instantly
• All data stored locally on your device
• No personal data collected
• Export and import data as JSON

The app uses RAWG.io database for game information lookup.
